I'm a geometer, working at the interface of algebraic and discrete geoemtry. I work in moduli theory and like computational problems.

For January-July 2024, I'm a visiting scholar at the Isaac Newton Institute, Universtiy of Cambridge.

Otherwise, I'm a postdoc in the research group Algorithmic Algebra of Prof. Peter Bürgisser, before that, I was a lecturer at Makerere University, Kampala.

I did my Ph.D under the supervision of Prof. Victoria Hoskins at FU Berlin.
